For one of my last hackathons of the year, I wanted to make a project that I can continue working on into the future, something that can do good in the hands of others. With the help of Mitchell, Matthew (another one, I know) and Jacky, we were able to make SafePulse. GutBuddy

— a dietary support app to recognize digestive disorders

githubwebsitefigmadevpostcanva

This was one of my most memorable hackathon experiences because I had the opportunity to be flown out to Berkley for their inperson hackathon, CalHacks! GutBuddy is a dietary support app designed to assist individuals in tracking and managing their gastrointestinal (GI) symptoms and conditions. It aims to help users better understand their digestive health and identify patterns or triggers that may be contributing to their symptoms. The app allows users to log and track their symptoms, meals, and lifestyle factors, and provides personalized insights and recommendations based on this data using machine learning.
